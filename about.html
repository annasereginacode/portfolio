Computer Science student at Lake Washington Institute of Technology with a background in Applied Mathematics and previous software development experience. Before returning to school, I worked on large software projects where I helped develop and improve web interfaces using JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and ASP technologies. That experience gave me an understanding of teamwork, long-term software projects, and practical problem solving.

Currently, I am continuing to build my skills through academic and personal projects focused on Java, Python, JavaScript, SQL, Git, and web development. I enjoy creating applications that combine logical thinking with user-focused design, and I especially like the process of solving problems, debugging code, and turning ideas into working software.

My main interests are full-stack web development and software engineering. I am interested in continuing to grow as a developer through hands-on experience and collaboration on real projects. In the future, I hope to contribute to software that is both technically strong and useful for people.

Outside of programming, I enjoy creative projects, exploring retro-inspired design ideas, and learning new technologies. I value reliability, curiosity, steady improvement, and clear communication. I believe good software development requires not only technical skills, but also patience, adaptability, and the ability to work well with others.
